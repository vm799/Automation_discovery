## HOW TO USE THIS SYSTEM

### FOR CLIENT ACQUISITION (Discovery Tool)

**BEFORE CALL:**
1. Open discovery-tool.html
2. Have Quick Reference Card nearby
3. Position laptop so they see you taking notes

**DURING CALL (25-30 min):**
1. Fill in company basics (2 min)
2. Document pain points (8-10 min)
3. List their tech stack (5 min)
4. Get metrics + vision (5 min)

**AFTER CALL (Same day):**
